JAJA OF OPOBO

From a boy sold into slavery to a king who ruled with power and pride. King Jaja of Opobo wasn’t just a leader, he was a force. The British tried to bend him, bribe him, break him.

He chose exile over compromise, dignity over betrayal. His empire fell, but his courage still stands tall. Some crowns aren’t made of gold, they’re made of fire.
