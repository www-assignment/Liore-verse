No Risk No Story

A story of democracy, resilience, and the price of standing for freedom

Image of Ken Saro-Wiwa

Leader • Visionary • Martyr

MKO Abiola

They begged him to step down. They threatened him, imprisoned him, broke his body. But Chief Moshood Kashimawo Olawale Abiola refused to bow. He believed so fiercely in Nigeria’s democracy that he staked his entire life on it.

And though he never lived to see it, his sacrifice became the seed that grew into our freedom. Sometimes the bravest thing you can do is stand when the world tells you to sit.

No Risk, No Story.
